## üìã Executive Summary
This project was a strategic initiative to mature the **information security posture** of a growing logistics company. Recognizing an evolving threat landscape, I architected a comprehensive **risk assessment program** to identify critical vulnerabilities and build a prioritized mitigation roadmap.

By shifting focus from "checkbox compliance" to a **Business-Centric Risk Methodology**, I provided leadership with a clear strategy to:
* **Protect critical revenue streams** and operational continuity.
* **Safeguard data sovereignty** in a hybrid-cloud environment.
* **Build long-term resilience** against sophisticated threats (Ransomware, BEC).

---

## üìâ 1. The Challenge (Gap Analysis)
The organization relied heavily on **cloud-based systems** for operations but lacked a formalized risk framework. Security was reactive and fragmented.

| **Vulnerability Category** | **Observed Gap** |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Data Governance** | **No Single Source of Truth:** Lack of centralized repository for evaluating risk. |
| **Control Efficacy** | **Ad-Hoc Controls:** Security measures implemented piecemeal without strategic alignment. |
| **Attack Surface** | **Unchecked Growth:** Rapid cloud adoption expanded entry points for adversaries. |
| **Resilience** | **Untested IR:** No formal Incident Response plan for major continuity threats. |

**Objective:** Transform security from a cost center into a **strategic enabler** of business growth.

---

## üõ†Ô∏è 2. Strategic Approach & Methodology
I designed an **end-to-end risk architecture** based on **NIST SP 800-30** and **ISO 27005**, tailored for practical business application.

### üîπ Phase 1: Asset-Centric Identification
* Conducted workshops with business unit leaders.
* Classified "Crown Jewel" assets: Cloud ERP, Customer Data, Financial Platforms.

### üîπ Phase 2: Threat Modeling & Mapping
* Mapped high-probability threats (Ransomware, Unauthorized Access) to specific technical vulnerabilities (MFA gaps, Mutable backups).
* Utilized **Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI)** to contextualize risks.

### üîπ Phase 3: Quantitative & Qualitative Analysis
* Developed a custom **Risk Matrix** incorporating financial, operational, and reputational impact.
* Translated technical risk into "Executive Language" (Impact on Revenue).

## üöÄ 3. Key Contributions & Results
Transitioned the organization from a **Reactive** posture to a **Proactive Risk Program**:

* ‚úÖ **Prioritized Roadmap:** Delivered 12 high-impact recommendations, enabling focused resource allocation.
* ‚úÖ **Identity Security:** Rolled out company-wide **MFA (Azure AD)**, drastically reducing BEC risk.
* ‚úÖ **Human Defense:** Launched a **Cybersecurity Awareness Program** with quarterly phishing simulations.
* ‚úÖ **Data Resilience:** Architected **Immutable, Off-site Backups** to neutralize ransomware extortion tactics.
